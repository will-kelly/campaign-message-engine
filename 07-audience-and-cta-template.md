## Filling tips

**If you can't fill in objections:** Pull 3–5 sales call recordings or 3–5 lost-deal notes. The objections will be in your own voice.

**If you can't fill in "what they ignore":** Look at your worst-performing content from the last 6 months. The patterns that didn't land tell you what this audience ignores.

**If the CTA bank feels thin:** Look at your last 10 published pieces and pull every CTA you used. Then ask which ones actually converted. Keep those. Discard the rest.

**If you have multiple audiences:** Create a separate copy of this file for each one. Name them clearly ("audience-CSM-leaders.md," "audience-CTOs.md," etc.) and upload all of them. The engine will pick the right one based on the campaign brief.
